Color Selection & Application Tip

Always test the selected grout color on a few joints before proceeding with the full installation — dried grout color may differ from the wet appearance. For wet areas and exterior applications, use polymer admixture instead of plain water for enhanced water resistance and flexibility. Clean excess grout from tile surface within 15 minutes before it hardens.
