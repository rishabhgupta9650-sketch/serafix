Key Benefits

Why Choose
SF-217?

✓
100% Waterproof — Zero water absorption in the cured joint. Unlike cement-based grouts, epoxy forms a completely impermeable barrier against water and liquids.
✓
Chemical Resistant — Resistant to a wide range of acids, alkalis, and cleaning chemicals — essential for food processing facilities, laboratories, and industrial floors.
✓
Stain Resistant — Non-porous epoxy surface repels stains from oil, grease, coffee, wine, and food — easy to clean and maintain in hygienic environments.
✓
High Durability — Exceptional hardness, compressive strength, and abrasion resistance ensures long joint life in heavy-traffic and industrial environments.
✓
Hygienic — Zero Water Absorption — Prevents bacterial growth in tile joints; ideal for food preparation areas, hospital operating theaters, and pharmaceutical facilities.
✓
Available in Multiple Colors — Wide color range to match or contrast with tiles, providing aesthetic flexibility for both functional and decorative applications.

Mixing Instructions

Mix Part A (resin) and Part B (hardener) thoroughly for 3 minutes, then add Part C (filler) and mix for a further 3 minutes until a uniform, lump-free consistency is achieved. Use the full unit — do not mix partial quantities. Work in small sections due to the limited pot life of 30 minutes.
