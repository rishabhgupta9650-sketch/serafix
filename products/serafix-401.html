Key Features

What Makes 401 PRO
Different

✓
C2TE S1 Classification — The highest EN 12004 performance grade for cement-based flexible adhesives. Exceeds all standard requirements.
✓
Handles tiles up to 120×120 cm — Engineered for modern large-format porcelain slabs including polished tiles, micro cement look, and stone-effect tiles.
✓
S1 Deformability — Transverse deformation ≥ 2.5 mm accommodates thermal movement, building settlement, and underfloor heating cycles.
✓
Extended Open Time (30 min) — E-class extended open time gives applicators ample working time for accurate positioning of large tiles.
✓
Non-Sag on Vertical Surfaces — T-class non-slip ensures tiles remain perfectly in position during installation on walls and facades.
✓
Suitable for Exterior Use — Frost-resistant and weather-resistant formulation for exterior facades, terraces, and cladding in all Indian climate zones.
✓
Compatible with UFH Systems — Formulated to perform over underfloor heating systems, absorbing repeated thermal cycling without bond failure.

Mixing Instructions

Mix 1 bag (25 kg) with approximately 7–7.5 litres of clean water using a slow-speed drill and paddle mixer until lump-free. Allow to stand for 5 minutes, then re-mix briefly before use. Do not add extra water.
