FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

How do I find a Serafix® dealer near me?

Contact us via phone or email with your city and state — our sales team will direct you to the nearest authorized dealer or help you procure directly.

Can I get a product sample before placing an order?

Yes — qualified contractors, architects, and builders can request product samples via the contact form. Our team will process your sample request within 5 business days.

Do you provide on-site technical support?

Yes — our field engineers provide on-site application support for large projects. Contact our technical team at viprabondingsolutions@gmail.com to arrange a site visit.

What is the minimum order quantity for bulk supply?

Minimum order quantities and bulk pricing vary by product and region. Please contact our sales team for project-specific pricing and supply arrangements.

How long does it take to receive an inquiry response?

We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours on business days (Monday–Saturday). For urgent queries, call us directly — we're available 9 AM to 6 PM IST.
