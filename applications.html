Residential
Flooring & Walls

Serafix® is the choice of professional tilers and home builders for residential projects. Our adhesives deliver long-lasting, beautiful results on all types of interior floor and wall tiles.

Living areas and bedrooms: SF 705 and SF 711 bond ceramic and vitrified tiles with reliable grip that lasts decades.

Wet areas — bathrooms & kitchens: SF 711 and SF 755 resist water penetration in wet area applications.

Living room feature walls: Non-sag formulation enables vertical wall installation without slippage.

Balconies and terraces: SF 755 provides highly-flexible bonding for outdoor exposed areas.

SF 735 / SF 755
Natural Stone

Marble, Granite
& Natural Stone

Natural stone demands a specialist adhesive. SF 735 and SF 755 protect the visual integrity of marble and granite while delivering superior bond strength for heavy slabs.

Italian and Indian marble floors: SF 735 and SF 755 provide superior bond for heavy stone without staining.

Granite countertops and flooring: SF 735 handles heavy stone weights without delamination.

Travertine and sandstone: SF 755 (S2 class) provides flexibility and extended open time for porous stone.

Decorative stone wall cladding: Non-sag, high-bond adhesion for vertical stone installation.

SF 755
Commercial & Exterior

Commercial Buildings
& Exterior Facades

SF 755 is the premium choice for large commercial projects requiring large-format tiles and exterior facade cladding. Its S1 flexibility class accommodates building movement without bond failure.

Exterior building facades: Weather-resistant, UV-stable adhesion for tiles and cladding on high-rise buildings.

Shopping malls and retail: Large-format porcelain tiles in high-traffic commercial areas demand superior bond strength.

Hotels and hospitality: Premium marble and stone lobbies, corridors, and amenity areas.

Underfloor heated systems: S1 flexibility absorbs thermal cycling movement without tile lifting.

SF 755 / SF-217
Wet Areas & Water Features

Swimming Pools
& Wet Installations

Water-immersed and constantly wet environments require adhesives with exceptional waterproofing and chemical resistance. SF 755 and SF-217 Epoxy Grout are engineered precisely for these demands.

Swimming pool interiors: SF-217 Epoxy Grout's 3-component epoxy system withstands continuous water immersion and pool chemicals.

Spa and jacuzzi installations: SF 755 and SF-217 resist hot water, chlorine, and salt in therapeutic water installations.

Water features and fountains: Durable bonding that maintains integrity in exposed water features.

Shower enclosures: SF 755 and SF-217 provide waterproofing under shower tiles.
