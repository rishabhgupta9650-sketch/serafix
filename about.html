The Serafix® Story

15 Years of
Manufacturing Excellence

We are the leading manufacturer of wide range of adhesives used for fixing various tiles and stones to any surface. Our adhesives have high strength that doesn't let any tile to loose its bond.

We also manufacture grouts, epoxy and cleaners that enhance the beauty of the tiles. We ensure and commit towards high quality products formulated using new standards and technologies.

Our products are in compliance with the Indian & European standards developed by the Bureau of Indian standards & the European standards organisation respectively.

Serafix® is the flagship brand of Vipra Bonding Solutions (VBS), headquartered in Delhi. Our products are manufactured as per IS:15477:2019 and EN:12004 standards and are trusted by contractors, architects, and builders across India.

Our Journey

Milestones That
Define Us

2009
Foundation of VBS

Vipra Bonding Solutions was established with a vision to manufacture world-class construction adhesives for the Indian market.

2011
Launch of Serafix® Brand

The Serafix® brand was officially launched with our first two tile adhesive products, meeting BIS quality standards from day one.

2014
ISO 9001 Certification

Achieved ISO 9001 quality management certification, validating our manufacturing processes and quality control systems.

2017
Product Range Expansion

Launched the SF 735 multipurpose adhesive and SF 755 highly-flexible tile adhesive to address growing market demand.

2020
Pan-India Dealer Network

Expanded our dealer network to 50+ partners across India, ensuring availability in all major construction markets.

2024–25
Next-Generation Formulations

Launched our SF 755 (Type-4T S2/C2TE S2) and SF-217 Waterproof Epoxy Grout, and established our online presence with Serafix.in.

Manufacturing

State-of-the-Art
Production Facility

We are committed to manufacture quality products — our manufacturing facilities and operational processes are driven by the highest standards of quality and excellence.

We have developed our product standards as per the Indian standards IS:15477:2019 and the European standard EN:12004. Raw materials are tested upon receipt and finished products are batch-coded for full traceability.

  • Automated gravimetric batching system
  • In-house testing laboratory — tensile, shear, slip resistance
  • Full batch traceability and documentation
  • ISO 9001:2015 certified quality management
  • Capacity for large-volume project supply
